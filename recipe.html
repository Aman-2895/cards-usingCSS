RECIPE OF GULAB JAMUN


Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups khoya,grated
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tsp maida

Steps:

  1. Boil ssugar and water on high flame
  2. Take the grated Khoya Add baking soda and maida
  3. Make a smooth and soft dough
  4. deep-fry Jamun balls
  5. immerse the gulab jamuns in the warm sugar syrup
  6. Hot and tasty sweet dish is ready to serve

RECIPE OF BARFI


Ingredients:

  • 3 cups milk powder
  • 1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  • 3 tablespoons butter or ghee
  • To garnish: Saffron, rose buds, and silver leaf

Steps:

  1. Heat butter and 1/2 cup of milk
  2. Gradually add in the powdered sugar
  3. Cook the mixture for 8-9 minutes
  4. Spread the mixture evenly on the greased pan

RECIPE OF LADDU


Ingredients:

  • 2 1/2 cup gram flour (besan)

  • 1/2 teaspoon edible food color

Steps:

  1. Make the boondi batter
